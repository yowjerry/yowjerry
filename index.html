Espace Egalité et genre en haiti

Espace Egalité Genre Haïti (EEGH) is a mechanism of the Haitian civil society established by several women's organizations working for gender equality in the country. The space aims to be a portal that facilitates and stimulates exchanges, shares and publishes docunents around gender equality, serves as a showcase for actions taken by women's organizations in Haiti and promotes their achievements, through the space's website.

I served as the backend developer on this project and was also responsible for the design and implementation of the database.
